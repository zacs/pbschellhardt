Welcome!

Welcome to the gallery website for P.B. Schellhardt. Throwing pottery out of her home near Jasper, Georgia, P.B. practices style X and style Y, and is similar in style to person X and person Y. She has recently been experimenting with alternative ways to fire her pieces, as well as mixing her own glazes.

This site is meant to let you take a look at some of her more recent pieces, and will eventually allow you to purchase select pieces. Feel free to get in touch if you have any questions.
